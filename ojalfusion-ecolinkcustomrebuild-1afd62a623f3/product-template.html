Categories

  2. Aqueous-based industrial cleaners and degreasers
  3. Solvent-based industrial cleaners and degreasers
  4. Solvent Substitutions HCFC-225/AK-225, NPB and TCE
  5. NDI, NDT, NDE ( pre- and post- NDT cleaners, magnetic particle inspection, liquid penetrant inspection, developers, removers, leak testing and emulsifiers)
  6. Surface Preparation Cleanroom supplies (tack cloths, swabs, pre-saturated wipes & canisters and lint-free, dry wipes)
Description
  • Aerosols include: flammable, nonflammable contact cleaners, defluxers, degreasers with high dielectric rating and multi-purpose applications
  • Aqueous solutions include: cleaners, conversion coatings, corrosion inhibitors, lubricants, multi-purpose, scale removal and strippers (photoresist and rust)
  • Solvent solutions include: degreasers/parts washing, flux removers, paint & epoxy strippers and wipe applications
  • Solvent substitutions include: HCFC-225/AK-225, NPB and TCE for degreasing, defluxing, drilling, multi-purpose and wipe applications
  • NDI/NDT/NDE include: Daraclean®, Magnaglo®, Magnavis®, MagnaVu®, Spotcheck® and Zyglo®
  • Surface Preparation, Cleanroom supplies include: tack cloths, swabs, pre-saturated wipes & canisters and lint-free, dry wipes
